Learn how to type the right way. Practice makes perfect.

Our project will record and evaluate every user’s hand position while typing by the webcams. Then, by selecting the important keys, we will be able to outline each key so that keyboarding helper can have a good idea of where the keyboard and each key is located. Our application will then evaluate the user’s hand posture while typing, which will then trigger our live feedback system, letting the user know their progress in real time.

View your progress. See for yourself.

Keyboarding Helper allows users to go to their respective dashboard. Our system analyzes the user’s typing and outputs various data visualizations. Such examples that are provided are mistakes distribution, typing speed, average correctness, and much more.

More convenient than classrooms. Easier and faster.

Imagine you are an instructor who is trying to teach 100 students how to type. It would be challenging to monitor every single student’s progress towards their hand’s posture while typing. Most, if not all, websites do not have a monitoring system for hand posturing. Keyboarding Helper aims to eliminate all these issues for instructors or for anyone who wants to learn how to type.
